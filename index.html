Brand colours

Use these exact values in all Yong Social branded materials.

Typography

Syne (display, headlines) · DM Sans (body, UI). Do not substitute typefaces in official communications.

Logo clearspace

Maintain a minimum clearspace equal to the height of the "Y" mark around all logo variants. Do not crop, rotate, or recolour the logo.

Usage permissions

Logos and brand assets may be used for editorial, press, and partner coverage. Commercial use requires written approval from Yong Social.
